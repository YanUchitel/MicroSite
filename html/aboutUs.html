About the Pioneer Corporation
Pioneer’s legacy began in 1930, when the founder Nozomu Matsumoto had a life-changing experience of listening to music reproduced from imported hi-fi dynamic speakers. He was so impressed with the soul-stirring sound that he decided to make great-sounding dynamic speakers for others to enjoy. Matsumoto’s passion and belief in sharing a thrilling sensation with others runs in the Pioneer Group’s philosophy “Move the Heart and Touch the Soul,” which has guided us in decision-making in business for decades. Other notable achievments:

  • December 1953: Hi-Fi Speaker PE-8 introduced.
  • June 1961: Company name changed to Pioneer Electronic Corporation.
  • June 1962: Introduces the world's first separate stereo system.
  • March 1966: Establishes sales companies in Europe and the U.S.
  • 1973: Introduces the reel to reel recorder RT-1020L
  • November 1975: Introduces the world's first component car stereo.
